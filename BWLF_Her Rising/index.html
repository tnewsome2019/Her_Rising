Mentorship

The association presently leads an annual 4-month entrepreneurship training program based on an in-house curriculum that combines life skills and socioemotional trainings designed to address the barriers to business endeavors faced by women and girls in Burkina Faso.

What have we done with your help?

BWLF is working to empower organizations to invest in their own capacity to fundraise and sustain their operations to ensure maximum impact for their communities. Through a two-year engagement, selected organizations will receive financial support and leadership guidance on skills related to organizational management, such as budget support, grant writing, monitoring and evaluation, and resources for effective program delivery. This support will help organizations more effectively participate in civil society and make an impact on improving the conditions for women and girls in Burkina Faso.
