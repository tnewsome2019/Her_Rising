Donate

Your donation can make a real difference in the lives of women and girls in Burkina Faso. By giving to Her Rising, you are supporting programs that promote education, leadership development, and economic empowerment. Every dollar you donate goes directly to support our work on the ground, helping to create lasting change in the lives of women and girls. Your support allows us to offer educational scholarships to girls who would otherwise be unable to attend school, provide business training to women who want to start their own enterprises, and offer mentorship and leadership development programs to help young women reach their full potential.

 

At Her Rising, we are committed to using your donation in the most effective way possible. We are transparent about our finances and are proud to share that over 90% of every dollar donated goes directly to support our programs in Burkina Faso. All contributions are tax-deductible, and we welcome donations of any size.

Thank you for considering a donation to Her Rising. Your support means the world to us, and to the women and girls we serve in Burkina Faso.

Volunteer

We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds who share our passion for empowering women and girls. Whether you have skills in education, healthcare, business, or community development, we can find a way for you to contribute to our work.

 

As a volunteer with Her Rising, you'll have the opportunity to work directly with women and girls in Burkina Faso, learn about a new culture, and make meaningful connections with people from all over the world. Depending on your skills and interests, you may be involved in teaching English or computer skills to young women, leading workshops on financial literacy or entrepreneurship, or providing healthcare services to communities in need.

Volunteering with Her Rising is a unique and rewarding experience that allows you to make a tangible difference in the lives of others. Our volunteers often find that the experience changes their own lives in unexpected ways, giving them new perspectives and insights that stay with them long after their volunteer service has ended.

 

If you're interested in volunteering with us, please fill out our volunteer application and we will be in touch with you soon. We appreciate your interest in Her Rising and look forward to working with you to empower women and girls in Burkina Faso.
