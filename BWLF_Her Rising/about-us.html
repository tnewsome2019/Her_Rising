Mission & Values

Her Rising is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering and supporting women and girls in Burkina Faso. Our mission is to create opportunities for education, leadership development, and economic empowerment that enable women and girls to thrive and reach their full potential.

 

We believe that every woman and girl deserves the chance to live a healthy and fulfilling life, free from discrimination and oppression. Through our programs, we work to promote gender equality, increase access to education, and create economic opportunities that support women's independence and autonomy.

 

At Her Rising, we are committed to building a more just and equitable world, one where all women and girls have the tools and resources they need to succeed. We are proud of the impact we have made in Burkina Faso, and we look forward to continuing to work with our partners and supporters to create a brighter future for women and girls everywhere.

Our Impact

At Her Rising, we are deeply committed to making a positive impact in the lives of women and girls in Burkina Faso. Through our programs and initiatives, we have been able to achieve tangible results that have helped empower and transform the lives of countless individuals and communities.

Here are just a few examples of our impact:

  • Education: We have provided scholarships, school supplies, and other resources to girls who might not have had access to education otherwise. Through our efforts, we have helped girls stay in school and pursue their dreams of a brighter future.
  • Leadership: We have created leadership programs that help girls and women develop the skills and confidence they need to become leaders in their communities. Through these programs, we have helped foster a new generation of strong, empowered women who are making a difference in their communities.
  • Economic empowerment: We have provided training, resources, and support to women who are starting their own businesses or pursuing other economic opportunities. Through these efforts, we have helped women gain financial independence and build better lives for themselves and their families.
  • Health and well-being: We have worked to improve access to healthcare and other vital resources for women and girls in Burkina Faso. Through our efforts, we have helped promote better health outcomes and reduce the prevalence of illnesses like HIV/AIDS and malaria.

 

These are just a few examples of the impact we have made at Her Rising. We are proud of the progress we have made, and we remain committed to continuing our work to empower and support women and girls in Burkina Faso.
