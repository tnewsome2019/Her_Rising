Our Programs

At Her Rising, we believe that empowering women and girls is essential to creating a more just and equitable world. That's why we offer a range of programs designed to promote gender equality, increase access to education, and create economic opportunities that support women's independence and autonomy. Our education program provides scholarships, mentorship, and educational resources to girls in Burkina Faso, while our leadership development program is designed to empower women and girls to become leaders in their communities. Our economic empowerment program focuses on creating opportunities for women to build sustainable livelihoods and achieve economic independence, and our health and wellness program promotes women's health and wellbeing through education and access to healthcare services. Through these programs, we are working to break down the barriers that prevent women and girls from reaching their full potential and build a brighter future for all women in Burkina Faso.

Programs

Education Program

Leadership Development Program

Our education program provides scholarships, mentorship, and educational resources to girls in Burkina Faso. We believe that every girl deserves the chance to receive a quality education, and our program helps to ensure that girls have the tools and resources they need to succeed. Through our partnerships with local schools and community organizations, we are working to increase access to education and break down the barriers that prevent girls from achieving their full potential.
Our leadership development program is designed to empower women and girls to become leaders in their communities. Through workshops, trainings, and mentorship, we help women and girls develop the skills and confidence they need to take on leadership roles and make a positive impact in their communities. Our program focuses on promoting women's voices and creating opportunities for women to lead and participate in decision-making processes.

Economic Empowerment Program

Our economic empowerment program is focused on creating opportunities for women to build sustainable livelihoods and achieve economic independence. We provide training and resources to help women start their own businesses, access financial services, and develop marketable skills. By promoting women's economic empowerment, we are working to break the cycle of poverty and promote gender equality in Burkina Faso.

Health & Wellness Program

Our health and wellness program is designed to promote women's health and wellbeing through education and access to healthcare services. We provide information and resources on topics such as reproductive health, hygiene, and nutrition, and we work with local healthcare providers to ensure that women have access to the care they need. By prioritizing women's health and wellbeing, we are working to create a world where all women can thrive.
