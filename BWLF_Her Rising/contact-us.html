Q: What is Her Rising and what is its mission?

A: Her Rising is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to empowering women and girls in Burkina Faso. Our mission is to create sustainable programs and initiatives that help women and girls gain access to education, leadership opportunities, economic empowerment, and better health and well-being.

Q: How does Her Rising support women and girls in Burkina Faso?

A: Her Rising supports women and girls in Burkina Faso through a variety of programs and initiatives. We provide scholarships, school supplies, and other resources to girls who might not have had access to education otherwise. We also offer leadership programs that help girls and women develop the skills and confidence they need to become leaders in their communities. Additionally, we provide training, resources, and support to women who are starting their own businesses or pursuing other economic opportunities. We work to improve access to healthcare and other vital resources for women and girls in Burkina Faso.
